Who are we?

Hello! Welcome to the Oconee County Fire Resuce (OCFR) website! OCFR is primarily a volunteer organization located in the heart of Georgia. We pride ourselves in serving our nearly 40,000 constituents with the best service and fastest response times in the state and plan to continue doing so. As a result, we require that our department volunteers be extremely well versed with what is needed to save structures and human lives. Interested in seeing our members, their certifications, or making changes for yourself? Then feel free to browse our site and make changes as you see fit.



Our Fire Chief

We are fortunate enough to have Dr. Bipin Prabhakar serving as the 13th Fire Chief of Oconee Country Fire Rescue. Since joining us in 2009, he has reshaped how our departments do business by assisting in raising taxes for new equipment and training in an effort to ensure we can best serve those within Oconee County and beyond.
